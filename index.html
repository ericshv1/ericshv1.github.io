About Me

I’ve loved baking ever since I was 5. Growing up, I found joy in crafting delicious desserts and sharing them with family and friends. That passion stayed with me through my childhood and evolved into what Ruby's Choco is today. Baking is not just a hobby for me; it’s an art and a way to bring happiness to those around me. Every cake, cookie, and treat I create is crafted with care, precision, and love.

I remember the first time I tried baking a chocolate cake with my grandmother, and ever since then, I knew this was my calling. Today, I’m thrilled to share my passion with everyone through Ruby's Choco. Whether you’re celebrating a special occasion or just craving a sweet treat, I’m here to make your day a little bit sweeter.
